Re-analysis using the approach described by Hamelin et al. (2023)

Figure 1a: t-SNE visualisation of quality-controlled single-cell RNA-seq data by cell line. This plot shows the multidimensional scaling of cells into a two-dimensional space after quality control and filtering of the raw data. Each colour represents a different cell line, demonstrating the clustering of cells based on gene expression profiles.

Figure 1b: t-SNE visualisation of quality-controlled single-cell RNA-seq data by tissue origin. The plot illustrates the results of a t-SNE analysis performed on quality-controlled samples derived from the author's processed raw data. Sequenced cells are categorised by tissue origin, with primary tumour samples shown in orange and cells derived from metastatic sites in blue.

Figure 2a: A Recurrence-Free Survival prediction graph for basal-like breast cancer using the upregulated transcripts found in supercluster A. The x-axis labelled as the time of recovery in months and the y axis is labelled as probability which measures the survival rate of each patient.
